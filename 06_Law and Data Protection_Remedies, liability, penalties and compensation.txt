WHAT TO COMPENSATE

WHAT TO COMPENSATE

• Both material and non-material damages
• Material damages

• GDPR  “the concept of damage should be broadly
interpreted in the light of the case law of the Court of
Justice in a manner which fully reflects the objectives
of this Regulation”

• are any "out of pocket" loss that are causes by a violation of the GDPR.
• usually forms of secondary harm
• (such as the loss of a job, the damage from having a contract denied or the
damage from price discrimination),
• are indirectly caused by a violation of the data subject's rights under
GDPR.

• Out of pocket losses can be objectively quantified.
